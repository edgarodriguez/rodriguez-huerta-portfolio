## (d) Synergistic Activities

1. **Research funding and leadership.** Principal Investigator and Co-Investigator on grants totalling approximately £2.1m, including a British Academy ISPF ODA Challenge-Oriented award (PI) on climate change, occupational health, and decent work in Brazilian agriculture, and several UK Home Office Modern Slavery Innovation Fund projects developing data science for decent work.

2. **Multilingual knowledge translation for frontline workers.** Developed worker-facing digital artefacts and training modules in English, Hindi, Punjabi, and Bengali, and the multilingual project website clidewo.com, translating supply-chain research into accessible resources for practitioners, civil-society organisations, and at-risk workers.

3. **Mentoring and teaching.** Supervised PhD, MSc (4), and BSc (3) students on food systems, data extraction, and regional development; delivered a graduate course on data management and visualisation for academic research; and led seminars on sustainability-science methods for measuring forced labour.

4. **Scholarly service and peer review.** Reviewer for *Journal of Cleaner Production*, *Energy Policy*, *Joule*, *Hydrogeology Journal*, *Anti-Trafficking Review*, and *Economía Creativa*; grant reviewer for the British Academy; and member of The British Academy Early Career Researcher Network.

5. **Interdisciplinary, cross-sector collaboration.** Built research partnerships with Tufts University (Friedman School of Nutrition Science and Policy; LASTING project), Stanford University (Center for Ocean Solutions), the US Department of Labor, and survivor-led organisations; team contributions recognised as a THE Awards finalist (Research Project of the Year in Social Sciences, 2024) and with a University of Nottingham Knowledge Exchange and Impact Award (2024).
